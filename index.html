Who we are?

Friends of Chokitu is a server created for various groups of my friends to have fun when they feel like playing Minecraft. The idea is to leave it open 24/7 in a dedicated host, and at any time a group of my friends, or a group of friends of a friend of mine can come in and try their luck against the guilds that play there, whether through PvP or Alliances.

Survival Life Steal

Server Idea:
Everyone will have a guild to play with (your friends), you will be able to explore with your group, create your village, protect your house, but be careful, if someone kills you you will lose a heart and when you reach 0 hearts You will be banned from the server until your group gathers items to revive you. There will be a place in the middle of the map for safer negotiations, this is the only place with Anti-griefing, and there will be some NPCs to defend people there, but be careful, as the guilds progress, the NPCs may not be able to handle it. What will your group's alignment be? Guardians of newcomers? Soulless killers? Choose what you want to do! I will not have any staff power within the game, to maintain integrity

Server FAQs.

The most asqued quetions and answers for you.

I don't know Chokitu, can I join?
Yes, of course, the server is meant to be friendly, so we try to keep inside our community, but you are more than welcome to join us.
Is it Vanilla Minecraft?
Yes and No, we tried to keep the game as much Vanilla as possible with some plugins to make our experience better. We might be adding new monsters and weapons in the future, if the community wants it, tho.
Can I play without a Minecraft Account?
Yes, we run on offline mode. You can even log in using BedRock (Cell Phone, Xbox or other platforms)
What Mods do I need to have?
You only need a mod to talk in game, using the VO-IP system, that can be found in our Discord. Besides that, you are good to go!
