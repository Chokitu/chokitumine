Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Be respectful: Respect other members. Do not bully, insult, say offensive comments, or anything that could harm another person. Flames and jokes are accepted, but nothing involving real-life situations. Any type of prejudice will lead to the person being immediately banned, including but not limited to: Racism; Homophobia; Transphobia; Sexism.
  2. Don't mess up the premise of the server: The server was created for us to have fun, so please don't do anything that will harm the gameplay.
  3. Don't Spam: Please avoid making personal announcements. This server is for us to have fun among friends.
  4. Don't abuse Bugs: The server may have bugs, and any abuse of mechanics is subject to a ban.
  5. Automating Totem of Undying farm is forbidden: The Totem is important in the game for crafting new hearts, and therefore its automated farming will be prohibited.
  6. It is forbidden to give hearts to team members: Unless you are negotiating for an item, if I notice that the most skilled PvP member has more hearts because their team gave them to gain advantages, the guild will be punished.

Discord Server Rules

  1. Always remain respectful to other people.
  2. Do not use slurs or defamatory language towards other members.
  3. Any language that may discriminate against other people based on sex, gender, race, etc will lead to banning.
  4. Do not threaten anyone on this channel.
  5. No advertisement on this server. Offenders will be muted. Live-Streams are permitted.
  6. Do not start unnecessary drama with other server members.
  7. Trolling and/or harassing other members is prohibited.
  8. Do not use abusive language on any member.
  9. Political or religious discussions are prohibited on this server.
  10. Be respectful to moderators and other members of our team at all times.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
